About Me

My name is Betty Chen and I was born in Manhattan, NY, but only remember most of my childhood spent in Buffalo, NY. After a couple of years, my family moved to Ridgewood, NJ, where I stayed until I graduated from high school. I then graduated from UCONN with a bachelor's in Molecular and Cell Biology. After that, I worked in a pharmaceutical industry for one year and then a chemical laboratory for two years. I am now learning how to be a web developer through Rutgers Coding Bootcamp.


I enjoy eating all kinds of foods, such as Korean BBQ and sushi. watching shows, reading webtoons, playing games, baking, listening to music, and dancing.
